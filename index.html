ABOUT ME

I'm currently a senior at Stony Brook University majoring in computer science and minoring in digital arts. I've been studying computer science throughout high school and college but have always had a passion for graphic design and problem solving, which led me to the field of user experience design. I've previously worked as an programming instructor at iDTech Camps' Alexa Cafe, instructing students in Java and Python, and was also a UGC Fellow at Stony Brook University, where I mentored and assisted teaching freshman students in their first year seminar classes.

When I'm not in class or working, I like to stay involved with clubs on campus. I've been a cabinet member of the Vietnamese Student Association (VSA) for the three years, where I've been Editor, Event Coordinator Assistant, and Vice President. I'm also a member of Alpha Omega Epsilon, where I organized and hosted professional development workshops as the former Professional Chair. In my free time, I like practicing yoga, cooking, and drawing.
