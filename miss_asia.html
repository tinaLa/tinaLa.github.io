MISS ASIA

Cultural Pageant

Miss Asia is the biggest event of the year for the Vietnamese Student Association at Stony Brook University, where women from different Asian backgrounds come together to showcase and celebrate their cultures through performances.

The theme of the event in 2018 was Crazy Rich Asians, after the blockbuster movie released that summer that broke records and marked a big milestone for Asian representation in mainstream American media. While designing the promotional material for this event, I recreated many of the motifs and design choices made in the original movie poster. The turquoise background and gold text were constant themes in my designs, as well as the flowers and peacock. I created the silhouette of the crowned woman in Adobe Illustrator and then used to create the finished products in Adobe Photoshop.
