ABOUT ME

Hi! Welcome to my website. My name is Tina and I'm a college student and aspiring UX/UI designer from NY. I've been studying computer science throughout high school and college but have always had a passion for graphic design and problem solving, which led me to the field of user experience design. (As a computer science major who geeks out over the layout, typography, and color palatte of her programming projects, it was only a matter of time.)

Outside of classes, I like to stay involved with clubs on campus. I've been a cabinet member of the Vietnamese Student Association (VSA) for the past three years and have held the positions of Editor, Event Coordinator Assistant, and Vice President. I'm also a member of Alpha Omega Epsilon, where I organized and hosted professional development workshops as the former Professional Chair. In my free time, I like cooking, practicing yoga, and drawing.
