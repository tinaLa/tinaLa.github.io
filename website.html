TINALA.ME

Personal Website

This is the website you are currently looking at! I personally developed this website in order to create an online professional presence and showcase my projects and work.

These are the initial wireframe sketches I made, laying out the general structure I wanted for my website. From the start, I wanted the layout and visuals of the site to be minimalist, straightforward, and simple.

After creating these rough sketches, I created initial digital wireframes with Adobe XD. The color and font choices I made for my site were intentionally made to coordinate with my resume’s color palette and fonts, which was created based off of a template.


I also designed a personal logo for my website, first by sketching and then rendering it digitally with Inkscape. The design is meant to be minimalist to fit with the overall feel of the website and incorporates geometric shapes. I chose bubbles as a motif in my personal logo because I have been told that I have a bubbly personality! “Bubbles” was also my summer camp instructor nickname at Alexa Cafe the past summer.

Using these designs, I developed the website with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
