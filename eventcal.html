EVENTCAL

Make School Summer Academy '17 Project

Eventcal is a social iOS mobile application designed to help friends make plans with each other through shared calendars. This mobile application was developed during my summer at Make School Summer Academy, where I was first introduced to iOS mobile app development and UI/UX design. The idea came to me while trying to plan a large reunion dinner with some old classmates who all had busy schedules.

This application was developed in Swift and implemented various Objective-C libraries such as JTAppleCalendar and Google Places. JTAppleCalendar provided a framework for a calendar within the app and Google Places allowed for more accurate location setting.
